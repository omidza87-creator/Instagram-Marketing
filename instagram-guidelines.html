üéØ The Two Pillars of Success

1
Content is King
Create valuable content that solves problems, educates, or inspires your audience. Quality over quantity always wins.
2
Consistency Wins
Post 2 times per week minimum. Continue for 3-4 months. Inconsistent posting (active for 1 month, then 2 months gap) doesn't work.
ü§ù Collaboration Rule: Both parties must be consistent for collaborations to work. If one person doesn't engage, Instagram may not recognize it as valuable content and block visibility.
